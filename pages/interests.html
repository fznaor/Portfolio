Other

I also like watching movies, although not as much as TV shows, so that's why they didn't get their own article. During the summer months, I enjoy frequently riding my bike across Osijek and its neighbouring places. As far as other physical activities go, I am too lazy for them. I trained swimming for 3 years when I was younger, followed by short stints of training waterpolo and tennis.

I know how to solve the Rubik's cube. It's been a while since I've practiced solving it, but my personal best is a decent 39 seconds. I also know how to solve the 4x4 and 5x5 versions, but I'm not that quick in solving them.

I was a member of the Javor Scout Club for 6 years. I loved being a scout because it gave me an opportunity to travel around Croatia (and sometimes even abroad) and meet new people. I was a leader of a troop for a year, but obligations in school and a general lack of motivation forced me to quit.

For the last couple of years, I have been going to pub quizzes regularly. Having loved learning trivia from a young age, it's not surprising that I've come to love them. My best categories are geography, science and (some) sports. The two teams I regularly play in are called Nestašni karcinomi and Osmijeh Franje Tuđmana. We have managed to win in 6 different pubs, but our most successful days seem to be behind us. I also like participating in individual quizzes such as the World Quizzing Championships. My biggest accomplishment is a 1st place in Osijek among quizzers younger than 23 years.
